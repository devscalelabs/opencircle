# Welcome to OpenCircle

OpenCircle is an open-source social learning platform that combines online education with community interaction. Learn together, share knowledge, and build a vibrant learning community.

## What is OpenCircle?

OpenCircle brings together the best of social media and online learning platforms. It's designed for creators, educators, and learners who want to build engaged communities around educational content.

### Key Features

- **Social Learning** - Engage with posts, articles, and discussions
- **Structured Courses** - Create and take comprehensive courses with lessons, quizzes, and assignments
- **Community Channels** - Join topic-based channels to connect with like-minded learners
- **Rich Content** - Share articles, posts, and resources with your community
- **Real-time Notifications** - Stay updated with mentions, reactions, and activities
- **Admin Dashboard** - Powerful tools for managing your community and content

## Who is OpenCircle For?

### Educators & Creators
Build your educational community, create courses, share knowledge, and engage with your students in meaningful ways.

### Learners

### Community Managers
Manage channels, moderate content, organize learning resources, and foster an engaged learning community.
