# Content Management

Learn how to moderate and manage all content on your OpenCircle instance, including posts, articles, resources, and user-generated content.

## Content Overview

Access content management from the Admin Dashboard to view, moderate, and manage all platform content.

**IMAGE PLACEHOLDER: Content management interface**
*Description: Screenshot of the content management dashboard*

## Managing Posts

### Viewing All Posts

Browse all posts on the platform:
- Filter by date, author, channel
- Search by keywords
- Sort by engagement, date
- View post details

**IMAGE PLACEHOLDER: Posts list**
*Description: Screenshot of posts management interface*

### Moderating Posts

Actions available for posts:
- **Edit** - Modify post content
- **Delete** - Remove post
- **Pin** - Highlight important posts
- **Hide** - Temporarily hide post
- **Feature** - Showcase on platform

### Handling Reported Posts

When posts are reported:
1. Review the report and post content
2. Assess against community guidelines
3. Take appropriate action
4. Notify reporter and author
5. Document decision

## Managing Articles

### Article Administration

Manage all articles:
- View all published articles
- Edit article content
- Delete articles
- Feature articles
- Manage article categories

**IMAGE PLACEHOLDER: Articles management**

### Article Approval

If approval is required:
1. Review submitted articles
2. Check for quality and guidelines
3. Approve or reject
4. Provide feedback to author
5. Publish approved articles

### Resource Moderation

- View all resources
- Verify resource links

- Track resource usage

## Content Moderation Queue

### Moderation Dashboard

Central hub for moderation:
- Pending approvals
- Reported content
- Flagged items
- Priority queue

### Taking Action

For each item in queue:
1. Review content and context
2. Check community guidelines
3. Make decision
4. Apply action
5. Document reasoning

## Content Guidelines

### Setting Guidelines

Establish clear content rules:
- Acceptable content types
- Prohibited content
- Quality standards
- Attribution requirements
- Copyright policies

### Enforcing Guidelines

Consistently enforce rules:
- Review regularly
- Apply fairly
- Communicate clearly
- Document decisions
- Update as needed

## Best Practices

### Moderation Principles

- **Be consistent** - Apply rules fairly
- **Be transparent** - Explain decisions
- **Be timely** - Act quickly on reports
- **Be fair** - Consider context
- **Be communicative** - Keep users informed

### Content Quality

Maintain high standards:
- Encourage quality content
- Remove spam
- Verify information
- Promote valuable contributions
- Reward good content
