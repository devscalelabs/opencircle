# Quick Start

Get started with OpenCircle in just a few minutes. This guide will walk you through creating your account and exploring the platform's key features.

## Creating Your Account

### Registration Methods

OpenCircle offers two ways to create an account:

#### 1. Standard Registration

1. Navigate to the registration page
2. Fill in the required information:
   - **Username** - Your unique identifier (lowercase, no spaces)
   - **Full Name** - Your display name
   - **Email** - Your email address
   - **Password** - A secure password
   - **Invite Code** (Optional) - If you have an invite code, enter it here

3. Click the **Register** button

**IMAGE PLACEHOLDER: Registration form screenshot**
*Description: Screenshot showing the registration form with all fields filled in*

#### 2. GitHub OAuth

For a quicker signup process:

1. Click **Continue with GitHub**
2. Authorize OpenCircle to access your GitHub account
3. Your account will be created automatically using your GitHub information

**IMAGE PLACEHOLDER: GitHub OAuth button**
*Description: Screenshot highlighting the "Continue with GitHub" button*

### Invite Codes

Invite codes are optional but provide benefits:
- Automatic joining of specific channels
- Access to exclusive communities
- Special permissions or roles

If your community requires invite codes, contact your community administrator.

## Logging In

### Standard Login

1. Navigate to the login page
2. Enter your **Username** and **Password**
3. Click **Login**

**IMAGE PLACEHOLDER: Login form**
*Description: Screenshot of the login page*

### Forgot Password?

If you forget your password:

1. Click **Forgot password?** on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for a password reset link
4. Follow the link to create a new password

**IMAGE PLACEHOLDER: Password reset flow**
*Description: Screenshots showing the password reset process*

## First Steps After Login

### 1. Complete Your Profile

After logging in for the first time, set up your profile:

1. Click on your profile icon
2. Select **Edit Profile**
3. Add or update:
   - Profile picture
   - Bio
   - Social links
   - Other personal information

**IMAGE PLACEHOLDER: Edit profile page**
*Description: Screenshot of the profile editing interface*

### 2. Explore the Timeline

The Timeline is your home feed where you can:
- See posts from the community
- Share your own thoughts and content
- Interact with others' posts

### 3. Join Channels

3. Join channels that interest you

## Understanding the Interface

### Navigation Menu

The main navigation provides access to:

- **Timeline** - Your main feed
- **Courses** - Educational content
- **Articles** - Long-form content
- **Notifications** - Activity updates
- **Profile** - Your personal page

**IMAGE PLACEHOLDER: Navigation menu**
*Description: Screenshot highlighting the main navigation elements*

### Post Types

OpenCircle supports different content types:

- **Posts** - Short updates and discussions
- **Resources** - Shared files and links
- **Articles** - Long-form written content
- **Course Content** - Structured learning materials

### Switching Between Modes

On the Timeline, you can toggle between:
- **Posts** - Social feed
- **Resources** - Shared resources and files

**IMAGE PLACEHOLDER: Mode toggle**
*Description: Screenshot showing the posts/resources toggle*

## Start Exploring

Now that you have an account, here's what you can do:

### 1. Explore the Timeline

Your timeline is the heart of OpenCircle:
- See posts from the community
- Create your first post
- React and comment on content
- Switch between Posts and Resources views

### 2. Join Channels

Find communities that interest you:
- Browse available channels
- Join relevant topics
- Participate in discussions
- Share knowledge with others

### 3. Browse Courses

Start learning:
- Explore available courses
- Enroll in courses that interest you
- Track your progress
- Complete lessons at your own pace

### 4. Read Articles

Discover long-form content:
- Browse articles from creators
- Learn from in-depth tutorials
- Bookmark articles for later
- Engage with authors through comments

### 5. Customize Your Profile

Make it yours:
- Add a profile picture
- Write a compelling bio
- Share your interests
- Connect with others

## Quick Tips

**For the best experience:**
- ✅ Complete your profile first
- ✅ Join 3-5 channels that interest you
- ✅ Introduce yourself with a post
- ✅ Explore courses in your field
- ✅ Engage with the community

**Pro Tips:**
- Use `@username` to mention others
- Add images to make posts engaging
- Ask questions in relevant channels
- Share valuable resources
- Be active and helpful
