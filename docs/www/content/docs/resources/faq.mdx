# Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to commonly asked questions about OpenCircle.

## General Questions

### What is OpenCircle?

OpenCircle is an open-source social learning platform that combines online education with community interaction. It allows creators to build educational communities, share courses, and engage with learners.

### Is OpenCircle free?

Yes, OpenCircle is open-source software licensed under AGPL-3.0. You can use, modify, and deploy it for free. However, hosting and infrastructure costs may apply.

### Who can use OpenCircle?

OpenCircle is designed for:
- **Educators** - Create and share courses
- **Learners** - Access educational content
- **Community Managers** - Build learning communities
- **Organizations** - Host private learning platforms

### How is OpenCircle different from other platforms?

OpenCircle combines:
- Social media-style interactions
- Structured course content
- Topic-based channels
- Open-source flexibility
- Self-hosted control

## Account Questions

### How do I create an account?

1. Navigate to the registration page
2. Fill in your details (username, email, password)
3. Enter invite code if required
4. Click Register
5. Verify your email (if required)

See [Getting Started](/docs/getting-started) for details.

### Can I use GitHub to sign up?

Yes, if enabled on your instance, you can sign up using GitHub OAuth for quick registration.

### I forgot my password. What do I do?

1. Click "Forgot password?" on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for reset link
4. Follow the link to create a new password

### Can I change my username?

Username changes may or may not be allowed depending on your instance configuration. Contact your administrator if you need to change your username.

### How do I delete my account?

Contact your instance administrator to request account deletion. Some instances may provide self-service account deletion.

## Content Questions

### How do I create a post?

1. Navigate to your Timeline
2. Find the post creation form
3. Type your message
4. Add media or links (optional)
5. Click Post

See [Creating Content](/docs/platform/creating-content) for details.

### Can I edit or delete my posts?

Yes, you can edit and delete posts you've created. Look for the edit/delete options in the post menu (usually three dots).

### How do I mention someone?

Type `@` followed by their username. Select from the autocomplete suggestions. The mentioned user will receive a notification.

### What file types can I upload?

Common supported formats:
- **Images:** JPG, PNG, GIF
- **Videos:** MP4
- **Documents:** PDF (for resources)

Check with your instance administrator for specific limits and supported formats.

### How do I write an article?

If you have permission:
1. Navigate to Articles section
2. Click Create Article
3. Use the rich text editor
4. Add title, content, and images
5. Publish or save as draft

Article creation may be restricted on some instances.

## Course Questions

### How do I enroll in a course?

1. Browse to the Courses section
2. Click on a course you're interested in
3. Click the Enroll button
4. Start learning immediately

### Are courses free?

Course pricing depends on your instance configuration. Most OpenCircle instances offer free courses, but some may support paid courses.

### How do I track my progress?

Your course progress is tracked automatically as you complete lessons. View your progress in the course interface or your profile.

### Can I download course materials?

If the course creator has provided downloadable resources, you can download them from the course resources section.

### Do I get a certificate?

Certificate availability depends on:
- Instance configuration
- Course settings
- Completion requirements

Check the course details or ask your administrator.

## Channel Questions

### What are channels?

Channels are topic-based communities where users discuss specific subjects, share resources, and learn together.

### How do I join a channel?

1. Browse available channels
2. Click on a channel you're interested in
3. Click the Join button
4. Start participating

### Can I create my own channel?

Channel creation permissions vary by instance. Some allow all users to create channels, others restrict it to admins. Check your instance settings.

### How do I leave a channel?

Navigate to the channel, find the channel settings or menu, and click Leave Channel.

## Notification Questions

### How do I manage notifications?

1. Go to your profile or settings
2. Find Notification Settings
3. Choose which notifications you want
4. Save your preferences

See [Notifications](/docs/platform/notifications) for details.

### Why am I not receiving notifications?

Check:
- Notification settings are enabled
- Browser permissions are granted
- Email settings (for email notifications)
- You're logged in
- Internet connection is stable

### Can I disable certain notifications?

Yes, you can customize which types of notifications you receive in your notification settings.

## Technical Questions

### What browsers are supported?

OpenCircle works best on:
- Chrome 90+
- Firefox 88+
- Safari 14+
- Edge 90+

### Does OpenCircle work on mobile?

Yes, OpenCircle is fully responsive and works on mobile devices. Access through your mobile browser.

### Is there a mobile app?

OpenCircle is a web application. There's no native mobile app, but the web version is optimized for mobile browsers.

### Why is the platform slow?

Possible reasons:
- Slow internet connection
- Browser cache issues
- Server load
- Large media files

Try clearing your browser cache or contact your administrator.

### How do I report a bug?

Report bugs:
- To your instance administrator
- On GitHub Issues (for platform bugs)
- In community support channels

## Privacy and Security

### Is my data private?

Privacy depends on your instance configuration. Generally:
- Your profile is public
- Posts are public or channel-specific
- Private messages are private (if supported)
- Admins have access to platform data

### Can others see my email?

Your email address is typically private and not displayed to other users.

### How is my data protected?

OpenCircle implements security best practices, but specific security measures depend on your instance configuration and hosting.

### Can I export my data?

Data export capabilities depend on your instance. Contact your administrator about data export options.

## Admin Questions

### How do I become an admin?

Admin access is granted by the platform owner or existing administrators. You cannot self-promote to admin.

### What can admins do?

Admins can:
- Manage users
- Moderate content
- Create courses
- Configure settings
- Manage channels
- Send broadcasts

See [Admin Guide](/docs/admin) for details.

### How do I access the admin dashboard?

If you have admin permissions, navigate to the admin URL (typically `/admin` or a separate admin portal).

## Troubleshooting

### I can't log in

Try:
- Verify username and password
- Reset your password
- Clear browser cache
- Try different browser
- Contact administrator

### My content isn't showing

Try:
- Refresh the page
- Clear browser cache
- Check if content was deleted
- Verify you're in the right section
- Contact support

### Features aren't working

Try:
- Refresh the page
- Clear browser cache
- Update your browser
- Disable browser extensions
- Try different browser

See [Troubleshooting](/docs/resources/troubleshooting) for more help.
