# Community Guidelines

Welcome to OpenCircle! These guidelines help maintain a positive, respectful, and productive learning environment for everyone.

## Core Principles

### Be Respectful

- Treat all community members with respect and kindness
- Value diverse perspectives and experiences
- Disagree constructively and professionally
- Avoid personal attacks or harassment

### Be Helpful

- Share knowledge generously
- Support fellow learners
- Provide constructive feedback
- Answer questions when you can

### Be Authentic

- Be yourself and genuine in interactions
- Give credit where credit is due
- Admit when you don't know something
- Share your real experiences

### Be Responsible

- Take ownership of your words and actions
- Respect intellectual property
- Protect privacy and personal information
- Report issues appropriately

## Content Guidelines

### Acceptable Content

**Encouraged:**
- Educational content and resources
- Thoughtful questions and discussions
- Constructive feedback and critiques
- Personal experiences and insights
- Helpful tips and tutorials
- Relevant news and updates

**Allowed:**
- Self-promotion (in moderation)
- Off-topic discussions (in appropriate channels)
- Humor and casual conversation
- Sharing external resources

### Prohibited Content

**Never post:**
- Harassment, bullying, or hate speech
- Spam or excessive self-promotion
- Illegal content or activities
- Personal information without consent
- Plagiarized or stolen content
- Malware, viruses, or malicious links
- Adult or inappropriate content
- Misinformation or deliberately false information

## Interaction Guidelines

### Posts and Comments

- Stay on topic in channels
- Use clear, descriptive titles
- Format code properly
- Search before asking duplicate questions
- Edit rather than delete and repost
- Use mentions thoughtfully

### Reactions and Engagement

- React to show appreciation
- Comment to add value
- Share helpful content
- Support quality contributions
- Avoid reaction spam

### Discussions and Debates

- Focus on ideas, not people
- Provide evidence for claims
- Listen to understand
- Agree to disagree respectfully
- Keep discussions constructive

## Channel-Specific Rules

### General Channels

- Keep discussions relevant to channel topic
- Follow channel-specific rules
- Respect channel moderators
- Use appropriate channels for different topics

### Course Channels

- Stay focused on course content
- Help fellow students
- Don't share assignment answers directly
- Respect instructor guidelines

### Support Channels

- Provide detailed problem descriptions
- Share what you've already tried
- Mark solutions when found
- Thank those who help

## Moderation

### What Gets Moderated

Content may be removed if it:
- Violates these guidelines
- Is spam or off-topic
- Contains inappropriate material
- Infringes on rights
- Disrupts the community

### Moderation Actions

Depending on severity:
- **Warning** - First offense, minor violation
- **Content Removal** - Inappropriate posts deleted
- **Temporary Ban** - Serious or repeated violations
- **Permanent Ban** - Severe or persistent violations

### Appeals

If you believe moderation was unfair:
1. Contact the moderator privately
2. Explain your perspective calmly
3. Provide context if needed
4. Accept the final decision

## Best Practices

### For Learners

- Ask clear, specific questions
- Show what you've tried
- Be patient waiting for responses
- Give back by helping others
- Stay curious and open-minded

### For Educators

- Create quality, accurate content
- Engage with your students
- Provide timely feedback
- Update content regularly
- Foster inclusive learning

### For Everyone

- Read before posting
- Search before asking
- Think before sharing
- Proofread your content
- Be patient and kind

## Privacy and Safety

### Protect Your Privacy

- Don't share sensitive personal information
- Use privacy settings appropriately
- Be cautious with external links
- Verify before trusting

### Protect Others' Privacy

- Don't share others' information
- Respect confidentiality
- Get consent before sharing
- Blur sensitive information in screenshots

### Report Issues

If you see something concerning:
1. Use the report function
2. Provide specific details
3. Don't engage with the content
4. Let moderators handle it

## Intellectual Property

### Respect Copyright

- Only share content you have rights to
- Give proper attribution
- Link to original sources
- Don't plagiarize

### Sharing Content

When sharing:
- Credit original creators
- Link to sources
- Respect licenses
- Follow fair use guidelines

### Your Content

When you post:
- You retain ownership
- You grant platform usage rights
- Others may share with attribution
- Respect the platform license

## Enforcement

### Reporting Violations

To report issues:
1. Use the report button on content
2. Contact moderators directly
3. Email administrators
4. Provide evidence and context

### Investigation Process

When violations are reported:
1. Moderators review the report
2. Context is considered
3. Appropriate action is taken
4. Parties are notified

### Transparency

- Guidelines are public and clear
- Moderation is fair and consistent
- Decisions can be appealed
- Community input is valued

## Updates to Guidelines

These guidelines may be updated to:
- Address new situations
- Improve clarity
- Reflect community feedback
- Adapt to platform changes

**Last Updated:** Check with your instance administrator
