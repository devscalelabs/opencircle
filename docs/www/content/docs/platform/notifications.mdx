# Notifications

Notifications keep you informed about important activities, mentions, and interactions on OpenCircle.

## Understanding Notifications

### What are Notifications?

Notifications alert you when:
- Someone mentions you in a post
- Someone reacts to your content
- Someone comments on your posts
- Someone replies to your comments
- Course updates occur
- Important announcements are made

### Notification Types

OpenCircle supports several notification types:

#### Mentions
- Someone tags you with @username
- Appears in posts, comments, or replies
- Direct notification to get your attention

#### Reactions
- Someone reacts to your post or comment
- Shows who reacted and with what emoji
- Grouped by content item

#### Replies
- Someone replies to your post
- Someone comments on your content
- Threaded conversation updates

#### Course Updates
- New lessons added to enrolled courses
- Course announcements
- Assignment deadlines (if applicable)

#### System Notifications
- Platform announcements
- Important updates
- Account-related alerts

### Notification Center

Access your notifications:
1. Click the **Notifications** icon in the navigation
2. Or navigate to the Notifications page
3. View all your notifications in chronological order

## Managing Notifications

### Reading Notifications

To read notifications:
1. Click on a notification
2. It takes you to the relevant content
3. Notification is marked as read
4. Badge count decreases

**IMAGE PLACEHOLDER: Notification detail**
*Description: Screenshot of clicking on a notification to view content*

### Marking as Read

Mark notifications as read:
- **Individual** - Click on each notification
- **Mark all read** - Button to clear all at once
- **Automatic** - Some mark as read when viewed

**IMAGE PLACEHOLDER: Mark all read button**
*Description: Screenshot showing the mark all as read option*

### Clearing Notifications

Clear old notifications:
1. Find the clear or delete option
2. Remove individual notifications
3. Or clear all read notifications
4. Keep your notification center clean

## Notification Settings

### Configuring Notifications

Customize your notification preferences:
1. Navigate to Settings or Profile
2. Find Notification Settings
3. Choose which notifications you want
4. Save your preferences

**IMAGE PLACEHOLDER: Notification settings**
*Description: Screenshot of notification preferences page*

### Notification Types Control

Enable or disable:
- **Mentions** - When someone tags you
- **Reactions** - When someone reacts to your content
- **Replies** - When someone responds
- **Course updates** - Learning-related notifications
- **System alerts** - Platform announcements

### Notification Frequency

Some instances allow you to set:
- **Real-time** - Instant notifications
- **Digest** - Batched notifications (hourly, daily)
- **Off** - Disable specific types

## Notification Delivery

### In-App Notifications

Notifications within OpenCircle:
- Notification center
- Badge indicators
- Visual alerts
- Sound alerts (if enabled)

### Email Notifications

If enabled, receive notifications via email:
- Mention alerts
- Important updates
- Digest summaries
- Configurable frequency

**Note:** Email notification availability depends on your instance configuration.

### Browser Notifications

Desktop browser notifications:
- Real-time alerts
- Even when tab is inactive
- Requires browser permission
- Can be enabled/disabled

### Quick Actions

From the notification center:
- **View** - Go to the related content
- **Reply** - Respond directly (if supported)
- **Dismiss** - Mark as read

- **Type** - Mentions, reactions, replies
- **Status** - Read or unread
- **Date** - Recent or older

## Notification Best Practices

### Staying Organized

- Check notifications regularly
- Mark as read to keep track
- Clear old notifications
- Use filters to focus on important ones

### Managing Notification Overload

If you receive too many notifications:
- Adjust notification settings
- Disable less important types
- Use digest mode instead of real-time
- Mute specific channels or users (if supported)

### Responding to Notifications

When you receive notifications:
- **Mentions** - Respond promptly if it's a question
- **Reactions** - Acknowledge appreciation
- **Replies** - Continue conversations
- **Course updates** - Check new content

## Notification Troubleshooting

### Not Receiving Notifications

If you're not getting notifications:
1. Check notification settings
2. Verify you're logged in
3. Check browser permissions
4. Ensure notifications aren't muted
5. Clear browser cache

### Too Many Notifications

If you're overwhelmed:
1. Review notification settings
2. Disable non-essential types
3. Switch to digest mode
4. Mute busy channels

### Notifications Not Clearing

If notifications won't mark as read:
1. Refresh the page
2. Click directly on notification
3. Use "mark all read" button
4. Clear browser cache
5. Try a different browser

### Browser Notifications Not Working

If browser notifications fail:
1. Check browser permissions
2. Allow notifications for the site
3. Check browser settings
4. Disable ad blockers
5. Try a different browser

## Notification Etiquette

### Using Mentions Wisely

When mentioning others:
- Only mention when necessary
- Don't spam mentions
- Be respectful of others' time
- Provide context in your mention

When you're mentioned:
- Respond in a timely manner
- Acknowledge the mention
- Provide helpful responses
- Continue the conversation
