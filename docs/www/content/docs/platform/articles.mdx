# Articles

Articles are long-form content pieces on OpenCircle where creators share in-depth knowledge, tutorials, guides, and insights.

## Browsing Articles

### Article Catalog

Access articles:
1. Navigate to the **Articles** section from the main menu
2. Browse available articles
3. View article cards showing:
   - Title
   - Author
   - Cover image
   - Excerpt or description
   - Publication date

- **Browse** - View all published articles
- **Search** - Search by keywords

## Article Quality

### What Makes a Good Article

Quality articles typically have:
- Clear, descriptive title
- Well-structured content
- Proper formatting
- Relevant images or media
- Accurate information
- Proper attribution

### Community Standards

Articles should:
- Provide value to readers
- Be original or properly attributed
- Follow community guidelines
- Be respectful and appropriate

## Writing Articles

### Can I Write Articles?

Article creation may be:
- **Open to all** - Any user can write
- **Restricted** - Only certain roles can create
- **Approval required** - Articles need review

Check with your instance administrator about article creation permissions.

### Getting Started with Writing

If you can create articles:
1. Navigate to Articles section
2. Click **Create Article** or **Write**
3. Use the article editor
4. Publish when ready

For detailed writing guidance, see [Creating Content](/docs/platform/creating-content).

## Troubleshooting

### Article Won't Load

If an article doesn't load:
- Check internet connection
- Refresh the page
- Clear browser cache
- Try a different browser
- Ensure article is still published

### Images Not Displaying

If images don't show:
- Check internet connection
- Disable ad blockers
- Allow images in browser settings
- Try a different browser

### Can't Comment

If you can't comment:
- Ensure you're logged in
- Check if comments are enabled
- Verify you have permission
- Try refreshing the page
