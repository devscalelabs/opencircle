# Timeline

The Timeline is your home base on OpenCircle - a personalized feed showing posts, updates, and activity from the community.

## Understanding the Timeline

The Timeline displays:
- Posts from users across the platform
- Updates from channels you've joined
- Shared resources and content
- Community discussions and interactions

## Timeline Modes

### Posts Mode

The default view showing:
- Text posts
- Posts with media
- Posts with URL previews
- Replies and discussions

**IMAGE PLACEHOLDER: Posts mode**
*Description: Screenshot of timeline in posts mode*

### Resources Mode

Switch to Resources mode to see:
- Shared files and documents
- External links and tools
- Learning materials
- Community resources

**IMAGE PLACEHOLDER: Resources mode**
*Description: Screenshot of timeline in resources mode*

### Switching Modes

To switch between modes:
1. Look for the mode toggle tabs at the top of the timeline
2. Click **Posts** or **Resources**
3. The view updates instantly

**IMAGE PLACEHOLDER: Mode toggle**
*Description: Screenshot highlighting the posts/resources toggle buttons*

## Filtering Content

### By Channel

Filter timeline content by specific channels:
1. Look for the channel filter option
2. Select a channel from the dropdown
3. View only posts from that channel
4. Clear filter to see all content

**IMAGE PLACEHOLDER: Channel filter**
*Description: Screenshot showing the channel filter dropdown*

### By Content Type

Depending on your instance, you may be able to filter by:
- All posts
- Posts with media
- Posts with links
- Discussions only

## Creating Content from Timeline

### Quick Post

Create a post directly from the timeline:
1. Find the post creation form at the top
2. Type your message
3. Add media or links (optional)
4. Click **Post**

**IMAGE PLACEHOLDER: Quick post form**
*Description: Screenshot of the post creation form on timeline*

### Share Resource

When in Resources mode:
1. Click **Share Resource**
2. Fill in resource details
3. Click **Share**

## Interacting with Timeline Content

### Available Actions

For each post on the timeline, you can:
- **React** - Add emoji reactions
- **Comment** - Reply to the post
- **Share** - Share with your network (if supported)
- **Bookmark** - Save for later (if supported)

### Reading Posts

Click on any post to:
- View full content
- See all comments and replies
- Access detailed view
- Engage in deeper discussions

## Timeline Organization

### Post Order

Posts typically appear in:
- **Chronological order** - Newest first
- **Algorithmic order** - Based on relevance (if enabled)

### Infinite Scroll

The timeline uses infinite scroll:
- Scroll down to load more posts
- New posts load automatically
- No pagination needed

### Refresh Timeline

To see the latest posts:
- Pull down to refresh (mobile)
- Click refresh button (if available)
- Reload the page

## Timeline Notifications

### New Content Indicator

When new posts are available:
- A notification or button appears at the top
- Click to load new posts
- Timeline updates with latest content

## Customizing Your Timeline

### Following Users

If following is enabled:
- Follow users you're interested in
- Their posts appear more prominently
- Build a personalized feed

### Joining Channels

Join channels to:
- See channel-specific content
- Participate in focused discussions
- Filter by channel topics

## Timeline Best Practices

### Staying Engaged

- Check your timeline regularly
- Engage with posts through reactions and comments
- Share valuable content
- Participate in discussions

### Managing Your Feed

- Join relevant channels
- Follow interesting users
- Use filters to focus on specific topics
- Bookmark important posts for later

### Creating Quality Posts

When posting to the timeline:
- Be clear and concise
- Add value to the community
- Use appropriate channels
- Engage with responses

## Mobile Timeline

- Timeline is optimized for touch
- Swipe gestures may be available
- Pull to refresh for new content
- Tap to expand posts

## Timeline Performance

### Loading Speed

If the timeline loads slowly:
- Check your internet connection
- Clear browser cache
- Reduce number of active filters
- Try a different browser

### Missing Posts

If posts don't appear:
- Refresh the timeline
- Check your channel filters
- Verify you're logged in
- Clear filters and try again

## Privacy on Timeline

### What's Visible

On the public timeline:
- Public posts from all users
- Channel posts (if you're a member)
- Shared resources

### What's Private

Not visible on timeline:
- Private messages (if supported)
- Draft posts
- Deleted content
