# Managing Your Profile

Your profile is your identity on OpenCircle. It's how others see you and how you present yourself to the community.

## Viewing Your Profile

To view your profile:

1. Click on your username or profile picture in the navigation
2. Your profile page displays:
   - Profile picture and cover image
   - Username and full name
   - Bio and description
   - Your posts and activity
   - Courses you've created or enrolled in

## Editing Your Profile

### Accessing Profile Settings

1. Navigate to your profile page
2. Click the **Edit Profile** button
3. The profile editor will open

**IMAGE PLACEHOLDER: Edit profile button**
*Description: Screenshot highlighting the edit profile button location*

### Profile Information

You can customize the following:

#### Basic Information

- **Username** - Your unique identifier (set during registration, may not be changeable)
- **Full Name** - Your display name
- **Email** - Your email address
- **Bio** - A short description about yourself

**IMAGE PLACEHOLDER: Basic information form**
*Description: Screenshot of the basic information editing section*

#### Profile Images

- **Profile Picture** - Your avatar image
- **Cover Image** - Banner image for your profile (if supported)

**Tips for profile images:**
- Use clear, professional photos
- Recommended size: 400x400px for profile picture
- Supported formats: JPG, PNG, GIF

**IMAGE PLACEHOLDER: Image upload interface**
*Description: Screenshot showing how to upload profile and cover images*

#### Additional Information

Depending on your instance configuration, you may also add:
- Social media links
- Website URL
- Location
- Professional title
- Skills or interests

### Saving Changes

After making changes:
1. Review your updates
2. Click **Save** or **Update Profile**
3. Your changes will be reflected immediately

## Profile Privacy

### Public Information

By default, the following information is public:
- Username
- Full name
- Profile picture
- Bio
- Public posts and activity

### Managing Visibility

Check with your instance administrator about privacy settings and what information is visible to other users.

## Your Profile Content

### Posts Tab

View all posts you've created:
- Original posts
- Replies to others
- Shared resources

### Activity Overview

Your profile may display:
- Number of posts
- Courses enrolled
- Channels joined
- Followers/Following (if supported)

## Profile URL

Your profile has a unique URL:
```
https://your-instance.com/@username
```

Share this URL to direct others to your profile.

## Best Practices

### Profile Picture

- Use a clear, recognizable image
- Keep it professional and appropriate
- Update it occasionally to keep your profile fresh

### Bio

- Keep it concise (1-2 sentences)
- Highlight your interests or expertise
- Include relevant keywords for discoverability
- Add a touch of personality

**Example bios:**
- "Full-stack developer passionate about open-source. Learning and sharing knowledge about web technologies."
- "Educator creating courses on digital marketing. Coffee enthusiast and lifelong learner."
- "Designer exploring the intersection of art and technology. Always curious, always creating."

### Username

- Choose something memorable and professional
- Keep it consistent across platforms if possible
- Avoid special characters or numbers if possible

## Viewing Other Profiles

To view another user's profile:

1. Click on their username anywhere on the platform
2. Or navigate to `/@username` directly
3. View their:
   - Public posts
   - Bio and information
   - Shared content

## Profile Actions

When viewing other profiles, you may be able to:
- View their posts and content
- See their courses (if they're a creator)
- Mention them in posts
- Follow them (if following is enabled)

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Upload Profile Picture

If you're having trouble uploading images:
- Check file size (usually max 5MB)
- Ensure file format is supported (JPG, PNG, GIF)
- Try a different browser
- Clear your browser cache

### Changes Not Saving

If your profile changes aren't saving:
- Check your internet connection
- Ensure all required fields are filled
- Try refreshing the page
- Contact your administrator if issues persist
