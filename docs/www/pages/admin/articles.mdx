The Article Management feature provides content creation and publishing capabilities.

## Overview

Article management includes:
- Article creation and editing
- Content publishing workflow
- Media management
- Categorization and tagging
- Content analytics

## Key Components

- Article editor interface
- Publishing workflow tools
- Media management systems
- Tagging and categorization
- Analytics and reporting

## Usage

Administrators can create and edit articles, manage publishing workflows, organize content, track article performance, and maintain the knowledge base.
